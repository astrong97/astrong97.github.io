About Me

I'm Alexandra Strong, a Computer Science student and soon-to-be graduate at California State University, Los Angeles (CSULA).

I enjoy learning new things in terms of programming, tech, and most of all, game development and design! Whether it be through class, YouTube tutorials, or self-taught, I strongly believe that no one is ever done learning.

I have a very strong passion for game design and development. There is so much creativity that comes with creating games, and that is something I admire about each game I play. I'm currently working on a Unity 2D project now, so please feel free to check out my progess in the "Projects" tab.

All together, I have gained knowledge in Java, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Python, C#, MySQL, and a bit of C/C++. I have experience working with Visual Studio and Unity, Visual Studio Code, React Native, agile development, Git, GitHub, and image editors such as Photoshop and GIMP.
