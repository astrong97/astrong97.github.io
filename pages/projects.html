Projects

Posted on 02/26/2022

A very special project of mine that I have been actively working on is my Unity 2D RPG. Currently untitled, it is going to be a game influenced by some of my favorite RPG's, with strong influences with other genre's of games such as life simulation and adventure.

What's particularly exciting about creating this game is that I can incorporate unique features, items, and characters that usually aren't in your typical RPG. Taking place in an urban setting (inspired by areas I grew up in), five characters from different backgrounds (inspired by the community I grew up with) will need to come together to find out what is destroying their city, and stop this evil with newly-gained powers. Character bonds will affect combat effectiveness.

As for the development progress, a decent amount of basic artwork is done. This is just enough for me to get some scripting and maps created. I was able to use C# to create scripts for character movement and camera movement. With a mix of colliders and layering, I've been able to create a small, simple, interactive map where the main character can explore.

My next steps are to create room transitions, dialogue, and an inventory system. I plan on getting a Udemy course soon on these topics to further my knowledge and get to working!

I plan on updating this page as I go, so please check this page often!
